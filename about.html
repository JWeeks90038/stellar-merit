Our Story

Welcome to Stellar Merit Statuary, where whimsy meets craftsmanship. Each piece in our collection is lovingly handcrafted from high-quality cement, designed to bring joy, character, and a touch of magic to your living spaces.

What started as a passion for creating unique garden art has blossomed into a small business dedicated to producing one-of-a-kind statues. From peaceful meditating dogs that inspire zen-like tranquility to wise owls that watch over your garden, every statue tells its own story.

We believe that art should be accessible, durable, and delightful. Our cement statues are built to withstand the elements while maintaining their charm for years to come. Each piece is handcrafted with attention to detail, ensuring that no two statues are exactly alike.
